The Rules

The goal is to get closer to the score of 21 than the dealer without going above.

  • Normal numbered cards scores are equal to their number.
  • Picture cards ( J, Q K) count as 10.
  • Ace counts as 1 or 11, depending on if 11 would bust the player.

Press hit for an additional card and stand for the dealers turn
The dealer must hit until they have a score of 17 or more and then must stand.
